### Mission vs Vision Comparison

| Criteria              | Mission                                      | Vision                                           |
|-----------------------|----------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------|
| **Definition**        | Promise of an effect fulfilled continuously for customers | Clear picture of a fascinating, jointly aspired future |
| **Aim**               | What are we here for?                        | Where do we want to go?                          |
| **Content**           | Purpose, task, activity and effect           | Desired future situation                         |
| **Effect**            | To push and guide                            | To pull and guide                                |
| **Time horizon**      | Today and forever                            | A certain point in the future                    |
| **Frequency of change**| Rare                                        | Periodically                                     |
| **Necessity**         | Indispensable                                | Conditionally dispensable                        |
| **Similar Terms**     | Why, Purpose, Massive Transformative Purpose | Aspiration, Moonshot                             |

### Building Blocks

**Mission:** Purpose, Customer, Problem, Solution, Unique Contribution  
**Vision:** Future State, Desired Impact, Guiding Principle, Scope, Time Horizon

---

## Lumie Mission Ideas

1. Empowering patients with their complete longitudinal health data in one private, portable place, while connecting that data to relevant clinical studies and emerging treatments through structured interpretation.
2. Connecting that data to evidence-based insights by interpreting clinical research.
3. Giving patients control to take their health story anywhere, including to any practitioner or chat surface.
4. Making research relevant by mapping studies directly to each patient’s real timeline.
5. Building the trusted infrastructure that lets health data and advice follow the user, not the app.

---

## Lumie Vision Ideas

1. A world where every patient’s complete health story travels with them, instantly accessible to any practitioner or AI they choose.
2. A future where clinical research no longer sits in journals but flows directly into the daily decisions of patients and doctors.
3. Patients arrive at every medical appointment already informed by the latest relevant evidence mapped to their own timeline.
4. Health data becomes truly patient-owned infrastructure – portable, private, and interoperable across every device, app, and chat surface.
5. The gap between emerging treatments in papers and real patients who could benefit shrinks dramatically.

---

## Final Vision Statement

**A world where every patient’s complete health story travels with them – private, portable, and instantly enriched by the latest clinical research and intelligent insights, accessible to any practitioner or AI they choose.**

---

## Strongest Candidates

**Mission:** The refined first idea (complete data + research interpretation) is the strongest.
**Vision:** The first vision idea (health story travels with the patient, enriched by intelligence) is the strongest.
