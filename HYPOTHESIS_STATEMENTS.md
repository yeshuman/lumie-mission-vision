# Lumie Hypothesis Statements

## Hypothesis-Driven Design Structure

**We believe that...**  
**will result in...**  
**measured by...**  
**within X weeks among Y users.**

---

## Hypothesis 1: Patient Ownership & Visit Preparedness

**We believe that** giving patients a single portable longitudinal health record  
**will result in** increased sense of ownership and reduced anxiety when preparing for practitioner visits  
**measured by** a 2+ point increase on a 10-point preparedness scale  
**within 6 weeks among 50 pilot users.**

---

## Hypothesis 2: MCP Connector Adoption

**We believe that** exposing Lumie's structured database via MCP connectors  
**will result in** users integrating it into their existing tools (Notion, Claude, etc.)  
**measured by** 40%+ of early users connecting to one or more external surfaces  
**within 14 days of signup.**

---

## Hypothesis 3: Practitioner Credibility of Insights

**We believe that** a rubric-based research interpretation layer  
**will result in** practitioners finding the insights credible enough to reference in consultations  
**measured by** 60%+ of surveyed practitioners rating at least one insight as "useful or actionable" on a 5-point scale  
**during a 3-month pilot.**
